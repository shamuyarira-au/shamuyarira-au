About the company

Patient capital. Principled ownership.

How we operate

Our principles

  • I
    Long-term orientation

    We measure success in years and decades. Our decisions are guided by compounding value, not short-cycle returns.

  • II
    Operational independence

    Each subsidiary is led by its own management team. The holding company provides resources and governance without micromanagement.

  • III
    Integrity in all dealings

    We hold ourselves and our portfolio companies to the highest standards of ethical conduct, transparency, and accountability.

  • IV
    People-centred growth

    Talent is our most valuable asset. We invest in the development of teams and leaders across every company we own.
